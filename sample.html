ABOUT US

GITAM University, located in the scenic city of Visakhapatnam, is known for its rich academic history, innovative teaching, and vibrant student life. Over the years, the university has built a strong foundation in education, research, and cultural activities. Our alumni have made remarkable strides in various fields, contributing to global growth.

Our Memorable Achievements

  • First place in National Science Fair 2023
  • Successful launch of our Research and Development Lab
  • Organized the largest cultural festival in the region
  • Awarded 'Best Campus for Innovation' in 2022

Alumni Network

Our alumni network is an integral part of the GITAM community. With thousands of successful professionals across the globe, our alumni continue to make their mark in industries like technology, business, and research. They play an important role in mentoring current students and fostering a spirit of collaboration across generations.

Sessions and Workshops

At GITAM, we regularly host interactive sessions, workshops, and seminars for students to engage with industry experts and thought leaders. These sessions help our students to stay updated with the latest trends and advancements in their respective fields, while also offering them networking opportunities with professionals and alumni.

Student Clubs

Our campus is home to a variety of student clubs and societies that promote creativity, leadership, and collaboration. These clubs include tech clubs, cultural societies, sports teams, and entrepreneurship forums. They provide students with opportunities to explore their passions and contribute to the community.
